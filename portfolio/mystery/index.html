Pipeline

  • Volatile prices, seasonal variety and unpredictable surplus create a fresh problem each week
  • ILP solver finds provably optimal allocations across multiple box tiers
  • Multi-objective optimisation for value, diversity, fairness and more
  • Validated against years of real historical packing data

Hyper-dimensional scoring

  • Multiple penalty dimensions score every box: value, diversity, fungibility and more
  • ILP penalty scores embed power curves with piecewise-linear tangent lines
  • HHI concentration measures effective diversity per dimension
  • Two-layer model caps flooding at individual and fungible group levels

Tuning to proof

  • Bayesian optimisation tunes scoring parameters against years of packing history
  • Cross-validated with repeated-run stability grading per parameter
  • Glass-box EBM diagnoses scoring gaps and distils new penalty terms
  • HITL preference ratings tune penalties via Spearman correlation
