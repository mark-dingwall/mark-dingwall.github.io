optimisation pipeline

  • Optuna tunes 14 penalty weights across 364 historical boxes and synthetic negatives, cross-validated per tier
  • ILP embeds the tuned scoring function via tangent-line linearisation of the convex power penalties
  • EBM glass-box models diagnose structural gaps the hand-crafted function misses, distilling gains back in
  • Packer survey (130 scenarios, 7-point Likert) adds rank-correlation signal to sharpen penalty interactions
